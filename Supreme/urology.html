Advanced Urological Care for Optimal Health

Urology is the clinical and surgical specialty that emphasizes on the urinary tracts of males and females, and on the reproductive system of males. At Supreme Hospitals, our highly skilled and experienced Urology Specialist is trained to detect, treat, and oversee patients with complex urological conditions.

The organs covered by urology incorporate the kidneys, adrenal glands, ureters, urinary bladder, urethra, and the male reproductive organs. Our clinic combines the management of medical (non-surgical) issues like urinary tract infections and benign prostatic hyperplasia, as well as state-of-the-art surgeries, utilizing advanced technology and personalized care.

Nephrectomy
