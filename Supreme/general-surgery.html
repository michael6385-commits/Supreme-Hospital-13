Surgical Excellence

Overview

The General Surgery Department at Supreme Hospital has highly qualified and experienced general surgeons who focus on a comprehensive range of abdominal procedures, including those related to the throat, stomach, small bowel, colon, liver, pancreas, gallbladder, appendix, and bile ducts. Our seasoned practitioners also address skin conditions, breast health, trauma, peripheral vascular surgery, and hernias, along with performing endoscopic techniques like gastroscopy and colonoscopy.
